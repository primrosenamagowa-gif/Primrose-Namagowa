About Golden Fork

A Journey of Culinary Excellence and Passion

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Golden Fork represents a commitment to fine dining and exceptional culinary experiences. What started as a dream to create a premier restaurant has blossomed into a beloved destination for food enthusiasts and families alike.

Our founder, Chef Marcus Sterling, envisioned a space where traditional techniques meet innovative creativity, where every dish tells a story, and where guests feel welcomed as part of our extended family.

Our Mission

At Golden Fork, we believe that exceptional dining begins with exceptional ingredients. Our mission is to source the finest local and international products, preparing each dish with meticulous attention to detail and profound respect for the culinary arts.

We are dedicated to creating memorable experiences that engage all the senses, from the moment our guests walk through the door to the final bite of our signature desserts. Every detail matters.
